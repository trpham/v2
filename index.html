Our Mission

With the rise of social media, circular reporting and echo chambers have become increasingly pervasive issues in news reporting. News sources have become less inclined to conduct thorough research to improve the accuracy of their content, and many news sources are now distrusted by more than half of their audience.

Project Integrity seeks to address this issue by aggregating news articles from a curated set of reputable sources. These sources have repeatedly demonstrated their credibility through well-researched information, and are trusted sources of news by the majority of readers across the political spectrum.

We hope that Project Integrity will enable you to browse the news without fear of inaccurate or misleading information, and expose you to a more diverse perspective of the world while maintaining the integrity of the content you consume.
